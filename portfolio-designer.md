# Project Overview

Build a premium portfolio website for a Business Intelligence Analyst.

The visual direction should be inspired by editorial websites, luxury magazines, art galleries, and portfolios such as Massimo Vignelli's website.

The visual reference is only aesthetic. The portfolio content must focus on data analytics, business intelligence, dashboard development, and decision-making through data.

The objective is to create a timeless, elegant, sophisticated, and highly visual experience that communicates analytical expertise and business impact.

This project will showcase:

* Business Intelligence projects
* Power BI dashboards
* Data visualization projects
* Business analytics case studies
* KPI monitoring solutions
* Sales dashboards
* Financial dashboards
* Professional experience
* About section
* Contact section

---

# Design Philosophy

The website must communicate:

* Elegance
* Authority
* Analytical thinking
* Business expertise
* Professionalism
* Trust
* Data-driven decision making

The visitor should immediately understand that this is not a graphic designer portfolio.

This is a Business Intelligence professional portfolio presented through a premium editorial visual language.
